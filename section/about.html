Mission and Vision of Lafang

Lafang want's to be the most sustainable restaurant in Lapu-Lapu City sourcing our ingredients locally, supplementing produce with herbs grown on our rooftop garden, and giving back to the community through delivering healthy food.

  • We make our famous food and sauce fresh by hand every day, we choose the best toppings and mix just the right herbs and cheese on every slice. Crazy good.
  • to make customers happy and to share our family’s authentic Cebuano recipes with the world.
  • to give our customers a place to celebrate life’s special moments by offering the best food, service, and ambiance in Lapu-lapu.

Everything we do is about you. From chefs who create exciting new flavors, to crew members who know exactly how you want your drink – we prioritize what you need to get you on your way.
